Rich Fudge Cookies

This recipe is for those of you who don't know how to make a sandwich

Serioiusly this is pretty basic

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Your choice of sliced meat
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce (optional)
  • Tomatoes (optional)
  • Condiments (mayo, mustard, etc.)
  • Whatever else you want

Steps

  1. Slap them condiments on that bread
  2. Put meat on bottom slice
  3. Cheese goes on meat, if desired
  4. Lettuce, if desired
  5. Tomato, if desired
  6. Whatever else you wanted
  7. Other piece of bread
