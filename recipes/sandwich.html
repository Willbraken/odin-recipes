Rich Fudge Cookies

This recipe is for rich chocate fudge & oat no-bake cookies, best eaten with a glass of milk

This recipe is a favorite of mine and it's always a thanksgiving & christmans favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 stick butter
  • 4 TBS cocoa
  • 2 1/2 cups quick cooking oatmeal
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, milk, butter, & cocoa in saucepan
  2. Boil for 1 1/2 minutes
  3. Remove from heat
  4. Add peanut butter, vanilla, & oatmeal
  5. Drop teaspoonful amounts on waxed paper
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
